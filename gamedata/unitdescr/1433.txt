The Kitsune is a fox spirit. It has the appearance of a silvery fox with many tails. It is magically powerful and able to take the appearance of a beautiful woman. The Kitsune is a trickster and often whimsical or even mischievous in nature. In human guise they may play pranks on unsuspecting travelers, aid lowborn peasants to greatness or plot the rise or downfall of kingdoms. Kitsune are stealthy and in human guise have the abilities of a spy. All Kitsune are powerful mages of Nature, but most have other magical skills as well.
