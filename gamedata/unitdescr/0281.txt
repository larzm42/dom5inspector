The Norns are strange, cowled hags of unknown origin. They divine and weave the fates of men and are said to decide even the fates of gods in their weaving. They have foreseen the fate and future of Jotunheim and have replaced the Gygjas as advisors of the Jotun Jarls. Norns are skilled in the ancient sorcery known as Seith and they are seeresses of great power who can can prevent bad events before they occur.
